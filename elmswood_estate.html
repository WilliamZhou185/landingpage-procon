Elmswood, the jewel in the heart of Keyborough

Discover Elmswood and you'll find a unique lifestyle right in the heart of Keysborough. With careful planning, thoughtful design and attention to detail, it's little wonder Elmswood is set to become the talk of the town. Come and experience Elmswood today.

Elmswood is an exclusive boutique community situated between established residences and the prestigious Haileybury College in Keysborough. Elmswood offers a limited number of premium lots on picturesque streetscapes, and you'll enjoy a relaxed way of life while being within easy reach of everything you'll ever need. It's no secret that this stunning new development will appeal to discerning buyers. So if you're looking for land, take a look at Elmswood, before these superb lots disappear.

    Green, leafy streetscapes and parkland on your doorstep, what's not to love? Elmswood offers a tranquil setting that will provide the stunning backdrop for your dream home. Just over the back fence is the exclusive Haileybury College with its majestic buildings and rolling greens, all adding to the feeling of space.

    The secret to a great community is careful consideration and thoughtful design. Where everything is planned with great attention to detail, to create a thriving new community that becomes the talk of the town. From your very first experience, you'll love the feel of Elmswood. With it's easy flowing streets and quiet cul-de-sacs you can enjoy an environment that is private and intimate.

When you return home after a busy day, you'll understand and appreciate what makes Elmswood so special. In terms of education you have exclusive Haileybury College as your neighbour, plus direct-to-your-door fibre optics means every home enjoys ultra-fast connections.


  • LOCAL KNOWLEDGE

    Elmswood is nestled in an already established area of Keysborough. Being in this highly sought after location means that everything you could want is within easy reach. On the weekend play a round of golf at the nearby Keysborough or Southern Golf clubs, or unwind with a relaxing walk along the Red Gum trail in the natural surrounds of Braeside Park or Tatterson Park. Plus the beaches of Port Phillip Bay are just 10 minutes' drive away.

    Of course, if it's retail therapy that you want then you're spoiled for choice. Major and specialty retailers are only minutes away by car. And you'll be close to the important things such as schools, kindergartens and the Chandler community centre. At Elmswood, it's all about making life easy.
