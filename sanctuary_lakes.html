An affordable, private, prestige community close to Port Phillip Bay and with easy access to the City and all parts of Melbourne

Sanctuary Lakes is just 20 minutes from Melbourne's CBD, and takes resort style living to a new level.

Set around an 18-hole Greg Norman-designed golf course and featuring 168 acres of lake, the estate boasts a selection of residential land in a stunning natural environment setting, next door to a bird sanctuary, nature reserve and Port Phillip Bay.

Outstanding homesites with golf course frontages, lake views and parkside locations are only part of the story. Sanctuary Lakes is a self-contained lifestyle enclave where residents are members.
